This book is about the Nazis winning the WWII and a little boy called "Faudiax" who lost both his parents due to the ruthless nazi soliders who killed them. He grows up to hate on the Nazi and dreams of destorying it, he joins the Nazi army one day due to being poor but and he was sent to the front line to fight against the Japanese. However after the war he builds his own empire "Faudiax Empire" and soon he becomes the man who freed the world and destroyed the Nazi Empire and bringing justice and peace back again to the world.
